This Privacy Policy explains how Godmode ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the Godmode app and website, and describes your rights and choices regarding your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the app:

  • Personal Information: such as your name, email address, and any other information you provide when you create an account or contact us.
  • Device and Usage Information: including your device type, operating system, IP address, browser type, and how you interact with the app and site.
  • Fitness and Workout Data: details about your workout routines, progress, and preferences that you log or generate through the app.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: we may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and to analyse how you interact with our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • Providing and maintaining our services, such as tracking workouts and recommending routines.
  • Improving and personalising your experience, including through analytics and research.
  • Communicating with you about updates, offers, and support.
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms.
  • Displaying limited advertising or promotional content, where permitted, and measuring its effectiveness.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate and improve our services.
  • When required by law, legal process, or governmental request.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, subject to confidentiality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We strive to continuously enhance our security practices.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your information, subject to local laws:

  • Accessing and updating your account information.
  • Requesting deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Withdrawing consent or objecting to certain data processing.
  • Receiving a copy of your data in a portable format.

Please contact us if you wish to exercise any of these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
