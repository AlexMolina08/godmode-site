Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings in the app or by contacting our support team.

Is my workout data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we protect your data.

Can I sync with other fitness apps?

Currently, Godmode works as a standalone app. We're exploring integrations with popular fitness platforms for future updates.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund policies depend on your platform (App Store/Google Play). Contact us for assistance with refund requests.
